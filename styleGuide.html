Brief

SPACEWORK

The company is an interior design company specializing in small and medium workplace and studio. It focuses on creating a nurturing working environment that maximize productivity and creativity.

what does the company do, or offer?

Spadework offers services in workplace and studio interior design. It provides consultation, interior design and architecture services.It also sells creative workspace gadgets and stationaries.

who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

A small company with 5-50 employees or design agency with following attributes: Values creativity and Open to untraditional interior design.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase product?

The company website contains a blog with inspiration for modern interior design and creative workspace gadgets. It inspires and attracts individual customers to their online store.

The testimonial from previous clients.

Unique and creative interior design approach.
